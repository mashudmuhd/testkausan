WHO WE ARE ?
GROUP CHAIRMAN : MR. SHIHAB KAUSON He is the founder of Kauson Group. Born in Kerala State ( Kannur Dist ) India in Aristocratic family . Obtained the basic academic qualification. He landed in Oman on May 8th 2000 and started with small scale business by the support of his sponsor Mr. Younus Ibrahim Salmeen Al Fazari. He proved himself his efficiency in business through out in different progressive parts . Successfully managed the small scale business in competitive market . He deployed efficient executive staff for the growth of business and formed Kauson Group of companies. Brought all the companies under one roof . Now the group is in going on in excellent position by the control of Chairman Shihab . Hala Group now turns to Kauson Group leading business group in Sultanate of Oman and UAE
